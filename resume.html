Bio

I am an international primary school teacher who specialises in teaching in a manner that nourishes the development of students' social, emotional, moral, and academic skills in balance. My biggest strength as a teacher is establishing a learning environment that is welcoming, friendly, and supportive, which encourages students to participate and lets them feel safe to experiment, take risks, and make mistakes. This emotional atmosphere is founded in the construction and maintenance of strong learning relationships.

Throughout my 9 years of experience as a teacher, I have also gained extensive experience in curriculum development and have mentored many new teachers. During this time, I have taught in a range of in-person, online, and blended educational contexts.
